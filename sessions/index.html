Sessions

Like other DrupalCamps, the presentations at our event come from you—members of the Drupal community. DrupalCamp Austin is an excellent opportunity to teach others about the interesting code, design, or client projects you've solved using Drupal.

We are looking for talks at different skill levels on a range of Drupal topics including:

  • Development
  • Design and UX
  • Business
  • Site building
  • gov/npo/edu

How to submit a talk

  • Sessions are placed into 50-minute time slots and you should plan on using the full amount of time. If you finish early, you can field questions from the audience.
  • Check out the submitted sessions below to view the talks that have already proposed.
  • The last day to submit a session will be on Sunday, May 19th at 11:59pm CT.
  • The proposals will be reviewed by the organizers and we'll post the final schedule during the week of May 27th.
  • Ready to submit a talk? Submit your session proposal.
