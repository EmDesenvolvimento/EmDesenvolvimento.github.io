About

DrupalCamp Austin is the biggest Drupal community event in Texas. Come learn about every aspect of Drupal with sessions presented by you—members of the Drupal community.

Program

Friday
All-day workshops (see below)

Saturday
Sessions, Half-day workshops, Keynote, BoFs*, after party

Sunday
Sessions, Keynote, BoFs*

* BoFs are "Birds of a Feather" sessions. We will provide a room for people to gather around a topic and hold ad-hoc discussions and presentations.

Workshops

Workshops are paid trainings in a classroom setting, which focus on specific topics and teach with a hand-on approach. We make them very affordable and are definitely the way to go if you want to dive deeply into a particular aspect of Drupal.

We're still confirming the workshops that will be available, so stay tuned!

Venue

Drupal Camp Austin 2013 will be held at the Austin Convetion Center.
